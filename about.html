Portfolio Concept

This portfolio's concept is a blend between 8-bit, sketch, and vinyl. Music is a big part of my life, so, I wanted to represent that here. Additionally, as a programmer, how can you not pay homage to 8/16-bit. The sketch thing is just something that scratches the brain for me. Also, I don't think anyone has ever combined these three themes for a portfolio website. So, consider me the first!

I tried to best portray the theme of music by adding vinyl components in some places. The projects page is supposed to evoke that nostolgia of flipping through the records in the store to find the one you want. If you somehow still haven't noticed, the cursor click shows pixelated music notes! Lastly, the individual project pages have a mix between Spotify and Apple Music's lyric system to caption each of the images.
